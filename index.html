History of Kenryu's Programming/Technology Journey

In 2022, I can program on many different language and have many knowledge.

  1. My first experience with programming was the Scratch. I signed up on it and that started the 5 years of journey.

    2017 Spring

  2. My father gave me the first personal computer, it was the decade old laptop(seemed to be windows XP era) which the chromium os installed and required to be plugged into the power for whole time. Ended its life with broken charging port.

    2017 Spring

  3. Read the article about the Raspberry PI, the opensource single board computer on the magazine and I bought it. (though I didn't have ANY knowledge of Linux OS)

    2017 Summer
